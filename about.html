Why Robotics?

Robotics use to making prototype so it will help the problem better. Better for problem solving, If students are given the time, space and purpose to build robots and solve open ended problems. Program building robots aims to help students develop logic building and critical thinking skills at a very young age. This kind of job is better done by robots than by humans. Most robots today are used to do repetitive actions or jobs considered too dangerous for humans. A robot is ideal for going into a building that has a possible bomb. Robots are also used in factories to build things like cars, candy bars, and electronics.
