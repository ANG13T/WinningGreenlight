Competition:

It is well established that Cornell wins more Student Competitions than any other college. Additionally, Cornell has taken this proven success to expand this experience to students across the globe in student competitions that it now creates. Based upon its success from the renown Obama White House recognized Cornell Cup competition, Cornell Systems Engineering is creating a new robotics competition for both K-12 and college students called Big Dreams to Big Screens (BDBS). In the Big Dreams to Big Screens K-12 and college students, like you, who are turning their robotic visions into reality compete to earn a featured cameo for their robot in an upcoming blockbuster movie!
